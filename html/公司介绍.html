Brand Foundation

Franz Sandner

In 1924, Mr. Franz Sandner established his own company in Germany, and with a warm and gentle tone and harmonizing-precision design, Franz Sandner became well-known worldwide for its excelsior manufacturing technique. After the Second World War, most musical instrument factories moved to Nauheim, which near the Frankfurt suburb due to the border redrawn between Germany and Czech Republic. The company started again like many other musical instrument factories and continued improving its excelsior workmanship.

Germany Boutique

Richard Schuh

In 1950s, Mr. Franz Sandner's daughter, Sonja, and her husband, Richard Schuh, took over the family business. In the 1980s, they began manufacturing violins and guitars, and expanded their musical enterprise to include pianos and wind instruments as well.

Global Brand

Dietmar Schuh

The third generation Dietmar Schuh led the company in 1980s. And in 90's the company decided to establish their own product line in China. In 2004, Chinese factory was set up in Shanghai, China.
